Title: View My Consult
----
Description: Planning, prototyping, and launching Canfield’s first telemedicine app.
----
Role: Product Design, Interface Design, Front-End Development
----
Summary: View My Consult is Canfield’s first major telemedicine app. We had no data and little feedback to reference at the start, but our CEO had a vision that got us prototyping quickly. I led the early-stage design efforts and ultimately created a design system for the engineers to use.
