A Unique Set of Limitations

The design had to:

  • Guide users through the signup and upload process.
  • Present complicated sets of data in an understandable manner.
  • Be translated from English into Chinese.
  • Perform well on the iPad as well as in IE7.
  • Follow Olay's brand style guide.
  • Be search friendly (as in SEO).

A tall order, for sure!

The Evolution

Together with Olayâ€™s product managers, we designed an experience to guide users from the homepage through the upload process and into their custom results. Aided by several rounds of focus groups, we iterated the design several times before we released the first live version.

Localized & Mobilized

The website is set up to be translated into both Traditional and Simplified Chinese. All text strings are localized into a single file, which feeds into the page templates using PHP Mustache templates. To closely match Olay's brand, custom @font-face fonts are used in the font stack. A few media queries make adjustments on tablets and standard CSS takes care of older versions of Internet Explorer.
